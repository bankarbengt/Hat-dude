The left guy without a hat

Maybe the best person in the world.

Here's a time line of the guys life:

  • 1914 - Born in Cresco, Iowa
  • 1933 - Leaves his family's farm to attend the University of Minnesota, thanks to a Depression era program known as the "National Hat Administration"
  • 1935 - Has to stop school and save up more money. Works in the Civilian Conservation Corps, helping starving Americans. "I couldn't afford the hats anymore", he said. "All of this left scars on me."
  • 1937 - Finishes university and takes a job in the US Hat Service
  • 1942 - Receives a Ph.D. in Headwear
With the Ph.d. he actually can afford hats, he just doesn't wear them anyways.
